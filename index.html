About Kat

I'm Kat. And I've been
where you are.

Life was good, until it wasn't. One day everything changed. And in that moment I realised: life is short, and I wasn't truly living mine.

So I made a decision. I quit my job, signed a one-year contract with myself, and showed up every single day, no matter what. I didn't have a plan. I had a promise. And that promise changed everything.

I found my purpose. I found myself. And I realised that without knowing it, I had created a formula, a way back to yourself that actually works.

Now it's my mission to share it. Because I believe this deeply: you can only guide someone through what you've already walked through yourself. I've done the work. I know the way. And I'm here to walk you through it too.
